Google Search
When you search something, AI guesses what you really mean and ranks results using machine learning models trained on billions of searches.

YouTube Recommendations
It learns what you like based on watch time, clicks, and likes, then suggests videos using recommendation algorithms.

Instagram / Facebook Feed
AI decides which posts you see first by analyzing your interactions (likes, shares, comments) and predicting what you’ll engage with.

Google Maps Navigation
It predicts traffic and fastest routes using real-time data and past travel patterns with ML models.

Email Spam Filter (Gmail)
Machine learning detects spam by learning patterns from millions of emails marked as spam or safe.

Voice Assistants (Siri / Google Assistant)
They convert your speech to text and understand intent using Natural Language Processing (NLP) models.

Netflix Recommendations
It studies what you watch, pause, or skip to suggest shows using collaborative filtering techniques.

Online Shopping (Amazon/Flipkart)
AI suggests products based on your browsing and buying history using personalization algorithms.

Autocorrect & Predictive Text (Keyboard)
It predicts your next word based on language models trained on huge text datasets.

Face Unlock (Smartphones)
AI recognizes your face using image recognition models trained to detect unique facial features.
